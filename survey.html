What is Final Expense Insurance?

Final Expense Insurance is a whole life insurance policy meant to cover funeral expenses. These policies typically have payouts that range between $5,000 - $25,000.
While the cost of funeral expenses steadily rise from year to year; you can remove yourself from the concern of burdening your family with such a high expense by acquiring a very affordable final expense plan.
